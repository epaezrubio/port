Competences

I like writting nice code: clean, scalable, based in design patterns. I am a lover of the well-developed code an I really enjoy when programming turns into a challenge. Any lack of skill does not stop me if there is a book that talks about that topic. Unlike other developers, I am not afraid of reading a specification, standard, RFT or whatever is needed to get deeper into a given topic.

Between my work tools must be a good IDE, a debugger, a fast and flexible text editor and last but not least, a command console. Of course coffee and whiteboards for painting are taken for granted.

I am a supporter of Open Source, and I use resources developed by the community when it is possible (except some cases impossible to change nowadays). Moreover, I am a great friend of frameworks when the requirements fit to its functionality. I loooooove the TTD developing, there is no better satisfaction in the world than seeing your tests returning OK.

To conclude I would like to say that I enjoy working in professional teams, and also organization techniques like Scrum
